# SaaS Risk Assessment & GRC Project

## Overview
This project demonstrates a structured cybersecurity risk assessment for a simulated SaaS environment using Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) principles.

The project includes:
- Enterprise-style risk register
- Inherent and residual risk assessment
- Control effectiveness evaluation
- Risk treatment methodology
- Supporting assessment documentation

## Objectives
- Identify common cybersecurity risks in SaaS environments
- Assess risks using a Likelihood × Impact methodology
- Evaluate existing control effectiveness
- Determine residual risk exposure
- Apply risk treatment decisions aligned with governance principles

## Risk Assessment Methodology

### Inherent Risk Formula
Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact

### Risk Rating Matrix
| Score | Rating |
|------|--------|
| 1–2 | Low |
| 3–4 | Medium |
| 6 | High |
| 9 | Critical |
